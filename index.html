I'm a postdoctoral fellow in the Pure Mathematics department at the University of Waterloo. My research interests are in complex and Riemannian geometry, manifold topology, as well as some areas of algebraic topology.

I did my PhD at Stony Brook University under the supervision of Claude LeBrun. After that I was a CIRGET postdoctoral fellow at Université du Québec à Montréal (UQAM), during which time I taught at McGill University.

I frequently use both Mathematics Stack Exchange and MathOverflow.

Last semester I ran a seminar on Kirby calculus.
